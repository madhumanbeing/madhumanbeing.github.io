Ottolenghi Roast Carrot Salad
  • Prep time: 30 Mins
  • Cook time: 40 Mins
  • Serves: 4
    • Salad Ingredients
  • 1kg Carrots (peeled and cut on lengthways angle)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil (plus 1.5 tbps to serve)
  • 1 1/2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 10g mint leaves
  • 5gm dill
  • 8 dried apricots (finely sliced, 70gms)
  • 30gm salted and roasted almonds (roughly chopped)
  • Salt and pepper
    • Chamoy Ingredients
  • 40gm dried apricots
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • 2 tsp sumac
  • 45ml lime juice (plus 2 tsp to serve)
  • 1 1/2 tsp Aleppo chilli flakes (or 3/4 tsp regular chilli flakes)
  • 1 small garlic clove
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • Step 1

    Preheat oven to 240C fan forced.

    Step 2

    In a large bowl, mix the carrots with the oil, maple syrup, 1 1/4 teaspooons of salt and a good grind of pepper. Spread them out as much as possible over two large, 40cm x 30cm parchment-lined baking trays and roast for 18 minutes, tossing the carrots and swapping the trays halfway through, until the carrots are nicely browned but still retain a bite.

    Step 3

    While the carrots are roasting, blitz all the ingredients for the chamoy with 1/4 tsp ofsalt in a spice grinder or food processor until a smooth paste.

    Step 4

    As soon as the carrots are cooked, transfer them to a large bowl with the chamoy, mix well and leave for 20 minutes to let the flavours come together.

    Step 5

    Mix the carrots with the herbs and sliced apricots and transfer to a serving platter, finish with the almonds and remaining oil and lime juice.
