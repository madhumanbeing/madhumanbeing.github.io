Frittata
  • Prep time: 30 Minutes
  • Cook time: 1 Hour
  • Serves: 4
    • Ingredients
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 tbsp thickened cream
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 tsp garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup whatever other veggies you want
  • 1 brown onion
  • 8 small cup mushrooms
  • Feta
    • Step 1

    Cook off the mushrooms and onion in pan on low heat until soft.

    Step 2

    Preheat oven to 180C. Prepare oven tray lined with baking paper then add eggs after lightly beating.

    Step 3

    Add all other ingredients while crumbling the feta into big chunks.

    Step 4

    Cook for 1 hour then serve.
