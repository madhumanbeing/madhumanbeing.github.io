Moroccan Chickpea Salad
  • Prep time: 15 Minutes
  • Cook time: 45 Minutes
  • Serves: 2
    • Ingredients
  • 1 can Chickpeas (rinsed and drained)
  • 1/2 Cup Olive oil
  • 1 Clove minced garlic
  • 1 heaped tsp Coriander
  • 1 heaped tsp Cumin
  • 1 heaped tsp Tumeric
  • 1 heaped tsp Smoked Paprika
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1 Red onion
  • Feta
  • 1/4 Pumpkin
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1/2 Sweet Potato
  • Fresh green salad veggies
  • Parsley
  • Mint
    • Step 1

    Preheat over to 200Â°C, bake chickpeas for around 40 minutes, tossing every 15 or so minutes. (Beware of spitting oil)

    Step 2

    Chop roasting veggies and bake alongside chickpeas

    Step 3

    Make green salad and stir chickpeas through while still warm, add chopped herbs, top with roast veg and feta.
