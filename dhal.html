Dhal
  • Prep time: 10 Minutes
  • Cook time: 1.5 hours
  • Serves: 4
    • Ingredients
  • 1 Cup Brown onion (chopped)
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Tbsp Ginger
  • 1 Small Carrot
  • 1 Cup Dried red lentils
  • 1 Small Tomato (sliced)
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Coriander
  • 1 tsp Tumeric
  • 1/4 tsp Cardomom
  • 1/4 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp Cayanne pepper
  • 2 Tbsp Tomato paste
  • 1 Tbsp Olive or Sesame oil
  • 4 Cups Water/Stock
  • 1 Small tin Coconut cream
    • Step 1

    Saute onion, garlic and ginger until translucent. (Add carrot midway through)

    Step 2

    Turn down and add spices.

    Step 3

    Add water/stock, coconut cream, tomato and salt.

    Step 4

    Bring toa low boil, turn down and simmer covered for 30 minutes.

    Step 5

    Reduce for as long as you like for a thicker dhal.
