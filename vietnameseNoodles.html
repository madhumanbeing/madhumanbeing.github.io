Vietnamese Noodle Salad
  • Prep time: 20 Minutes
  • Cook time: 10 Minutes
  • Serves: 2
    • Nuoc Cham Sauce Ingredients
  • 3 Cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 1 Lime (juiced)
  • 2 Tbsp Rice wine vinegar
  • 1/4 Cup Fish sauce
  • 3 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1 Red chilli (sliced)
  • 1/2 Cup Cold water
  • Sesame oil (optional)
    • Marinade Ingredients
  • 2 Chicken Breasts or 4 Thighs
  • 2 Cloves Minced garlic
  • 1 Lime (juiced)
  • 2 Tbsp Fish sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Brown sugar
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
    • Salad Ingredients
  • 1/2 Cucumber
  • 1 Carrot
  • Peanuts (roughly chopped)
  • 1 Pack vermicelli noodles
  • 1/4 Red onion
    • Step 1

    Boil noodles for 3 minutes then strain and rinse with cold water.

    Step 2

    Cook chicken in pan until golden on outside.

    Step 3

    Cut cucumber, red onion and carrot into small strips.

    Step 4

    Mix everything but chicken in bowl and add Nuoc Cham sauce.

    Step 5

    Portion salad out, add chicken to top then sprinkle peanuts over.
